The process — five steps, one sitting each

How it works

Two of these steps happen on LinkedIn's own site. We only take over once you already have the file in hand.

Request your archive

On LinkedIn: Settings & Privacy → Data privacy → Get a copy of your data → "Download larger data archive" → Request archive.

On linkedin.com — we walk you through every click

Wait for LinkedIn's email

LinkedIn — not us — emails you directly the moment your archive is ready, anywhere from ten minutes to a few days later. We never see that email address; there's nothing for Copy of Record to send.

Typical wait: 10 min – 72 hrs · sent by linkedin.com

Download the zip

Save the file LinkedIn sends you. Don't unzip it — the toolkit reads it directly, exactly as it arrives.

One .zip file, no editing needed

Drop it into the toolkit

Open Copy of Record and drag the zip in. Everything runs as JavaScript in your own browser tab — nothing is transmitted anywhere.

Processing time: under a minute

Keep your files

Five files are generated on your machine as one zip. Save them, open them offline, forever — no login, no expiry.

Files are yours — re-run anytime for free

The privacy terms, plainly

What we never do

Never see it
Your archive is read by your browser and never transmitted to any server — including ours.
Never store it
Nothing about your connections, employer history, or messages is logged, cached, or retained anywhere.
Never require login
No account, no LinkedIn OAuth, no password. Payment unlocks the tool page — that's the whole relationship.
Never touch your account
We don't automate LinkedIn, request data on your behalf, or ask for your credentials. You click every button yourself, on linkedin.com.
Read-only, always
The toolkit only reads the archive you give it. It never writes back to LinkedIn, changes your profile, or contacts LinkedIn's servers in any way.
Only as complete as your export
Every number in your report is a direct read of what LinkedIn included in your archive — nothing is added, inferred, or independently verified. If a category is thin, delayed, or missing on LinkedIn's end, your report will reflect that too.

Before you ask

Questions on file

Is this allowed by LinkedIn?

Yes. You're using LinkedIn's own official export feature — the same one required under GDPR and CCPA. We never scrape your profile, log in on your behalf, or automate your account in any way.

Do you ever see my data?

No. The toolkit is a page of JavaScript that runs in your browser. Your zip file is read from your disk and processed there — it's never uploaded, and we have no server that could receive it.

What if a file is missing from my export?

LinkedIn lets you choose which categories to include. The toolkit builds around whatever you provide — skip a category, lose that one panel, everything else still works.

Can I run it again later?

Yes. Request a fresh archive from LinkedIn anytime and re-run the same tool page at no extra cost — your purchase isn't tied to a single export.
